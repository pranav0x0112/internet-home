🎉 GitHub-Style Admonitions Live Demo

Your Anna SSG now supports admonitions! Here's what they look like:

Standard Types

Note

This is a note admonition. Use this for general informational content that readers should know.

Tip

This is a tip. Great for sharing best practices, helpful hints, and pro tips.

Important

This is important information that readers should pay attention to and remember.

Warning

This warns readers about potential issues, gotchas, and things to be careful about.

Caution

This cautions about dangerous operations, critical mistakes, and critical warnings to avoid.

Custom Types

Fun Fact

You're not limited to standard types! Any [!CustomName] format becomes a custom admonition with default styling.

Pro Tip

Custom types automatically normalize the title. "Pro Tip" becomes properly formatted!

How to Use

Just write in your markdown:

> [!NOTE]
> Your content here

> [!TIP]
> Multi-line admonitions work too!
>
> Just continue with the > marker.

> [!CustomType]
> Any custom type you want!
